Privacy & Data Sovereignty Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025 Current Version

At The ECAD Bridge, we believe transparency is a fundamental truth. We are committed to the ethical stewardship of the data you entrust to us, ensuring that your professional privacy is maintained while providing the highest level of technical insight.

1. Data Synthesis (What we collect)

To facilitate our mission of technical excellence, we aggregate the following information:

  • Professional Identity: Your name, corporate affiliation, and electronic contact credentials.
  • Digital Interaction: Metadata regarding your activity within our digital ecosystem.
  • Assessment Intelligence: Specific data points and choices made within our Assessment Center to generate your technical profiles.

2. Acquisition of Information (How we collect it)

We collect data through direct engagement and transparent interaction. You provide this information when you:

  • Enroll in our global research initiatives.
  • Engage with our Assessment Center for professional benchmarking.
  • Register for access to our exclusive technical frameworks.

3. Strategic Use of Data

The ECAD Bridge utilizes your information to:

  • Orchestrate specialized communications, including research findings and survey invitations.
  • Personalize your journey through the Assessment Center to ensure maximum efficacy.
  • Facilitate Industry Connections: With your explicit consent, we may share insights with Assessment Center Sponsors to provide you with access to advanced tools and services. You maintain the right to opt-out of these communications at any time.

4. Data Stewardship & Retention

Your information is securely sequestered within our HubSpot management environment. To maintain the integrity of our historical research and longitudinal studies, The ECAD Bridge retains professional and activity data indefinitely, or until a request for erasure is invoked.

5. Your Data Sovereignty (Rights)

In alignment with global privacy standards, every member of our ecosystem is entitled to:

  • The Right to Access: Requesting a comprehensive record of your personal data.
  • The Right to Rectification: Ensuring all professional information is accurate and complete.
  • The Right to Erasure: Requesting the deletion of your data under specific conditions.
  • The Right to Restriction & Objection: Managing how and why your data is processed.
  • The Right to Portability: Transferring your data assets to other organizations or yourself.

Note: We respond to all governance requests within one calendar month. Contact us at ecadbridge@outlook.com to exercise these rights.

6. Digital Identifiers (Cookies)

We utilize cookies—small digital signatures—to optimize site architecture and recognize your preferences. These allow us to:

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the Assessment Center experience.
  • Maintain your functional preferences (such as language and localization).

You may disable cookies via your browser settings, though some advanced site capabilities may be diminished.

7. Jurisdictional Scope

Our policy applies exclusively to The ECAD Bridge. While our platform may contain links to external partners, we encourage you to review their respective policies upon departure from our site.

8. Governance Contact

For inquiries regarding our privacy standards or data integrity, please contact our administration:

Email

ecadbridge@outlook.com
